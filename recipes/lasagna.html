About Lasagna

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish that has delighted diners for centuries, originating in the Emilia-Romagna region.

It is a hearty and deeply satisfying baked pasta creation, traditionally constructed from alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich tomato-based ragù (meat sauce), and creamy Béchamel sauce.

The dish is typically assembled in a rectangular pan and baked until the top is golden brown and bubbling, allowing the distinct flavours to meld into a comforting, flavourful masterpiece. Though many regional and modern variations exist, the traditional preparation remains an enduring symbol of Italian comfort food.
